About Us

Con Artist is an art collective, community, workspace & gallery. Our shared venue and studio hosts events, collaborative exhibitions, group projects, life drawing, and more! We are a platform for artists to network, gain exposure, and share skills and knowledge with one another. Joining the Con Artist Collective means becoming part of an active community of over 500 creatives from all walks of life, and all over the world.

Why join our art collective?


Membership

Many members join the collective to be a part of an active community of creatives that curate exhibitions, put on regular member social events, frequently collaborate and so much more.


Workspace Access

One of the main reasons members join is to gain access to our creative workspace. Our space is currently equipped with various tools such as a 4-color silk-screen press, light-table, a power washer, a drop sink, communal supplies, computers, hardware, software and access to photo studios.

On top of this, members have the opportunity to sell their work through our website, in our gallery shows, in our storefront and also the various flea-market stands and international art fairs like Art Basel Miami, that we participate in.
